## 🔍 What is fintracker?

fintracker is a personal finance tracker app. It lets you add your income and expenses so you can see where your money goes. Use it to plan your budget, avoid overspending, and understand your financial habits better. The app works directly on your PC, with a clear interface that anyone can use.

Key features include:  
- Add income and expenses easily  
- View summaries by day, week, or month  
- Categorize your transactions (e.g., food, bills, salary)  
- Sign in safely using your Google account  
- Store your data securely with Firebase  
- Fast and responsive interface built on React and Vite
